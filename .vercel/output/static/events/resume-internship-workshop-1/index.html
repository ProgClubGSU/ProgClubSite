Event Details

workshop Resume

Get ready to boost your internship applications! This hands-on workshop covers everything from resume optimization to interview prep. What you'll learn:

  • How to make your resume stand out to recruiters
  • Tips for highlighting your programming projects
  • Networking strategies for tech companies
  • Interview preparation and common questions
  • Application timeline planning

Perfect for: Students seeking software engineering internships at any level.

Bring: Your current resume and laptop for hands-on work.

This is a collaborative session where you'll get personalized feedback and leave with actionable next steps. No experience required - just bring your enthusiasm and questions!

Event Recap

We had an amazing first resume/internship session! Here's what we covered:

  • Resume fundamentals - proper formatting and structure
  • Self-marketing strategies - how to write compelling titles and bullet points
  • FAANG recruiter insights - what top tech companies look for in interns
  • Networking techniques - how to connect with industry professionals
  • Referral process - leveraging connections to get your foot in the door

Thanks to everyone who came out! The energy was great and we covered a lot of ground. Stay tuned for more career development workshops coming soon.
